# Design System — Instagram Cards

## Visual Identity

**Style:** Bold typographic SMM cards. Instagram-first (1:1 square). Russian text unless specified.

**Mood:** Confident, editorial, modern. Not generic "AI-style". Strong brand presence.

## Color Palettes

### Palette A — Bright Blue (energy, action)
- Background: `#3C3FE8` (vivid electric blue)
- Text: `#FFFFFF`
- Accent: `#FFFFFF` pill badges, `#FFE600` highlights optional
- Use for: cover cards, CTAs, announcements

### Palette B — Dark Navy (authority, expertise)
- Background: `#0D1E5A` (deep navy)
- Text: `#FFFFFF`
- Accent: `#4F7FFF` (light blue for secondary text), white badges
- Use for: educational content, step-by-step, informational

### Palette C — Light / White (clean, readable)
- Background: `#F5F5F5` or `#FFFFFF`
- Text: `#0D1E5A` (navy)
- Accent: `#3C3FE8` (blue) for headings or badges
- Use for: text-heavy cards, quotes, definitions

### Palette D — Split (contrast, dynamic)
- Left/top: `#3C3FE8` blue, Right/bottom: `#FFFFFF` white
- Bold diagonal or hard edge split
- Use for: VS comparisons, before/after, feature reveals

## Typography

- **Headings:** Extra Bold / Black weight, uppercase (ALL CAPS), condensed
  - Huge — fills most of the card
  - Line height tight: ~0.9–1.0
- **Subheadings:** Medium weight, normal/sentence case, 40–50% of heading size
- **Body text:** Regular weight, small, muted color or gray
- **Labels/badges:** Tiny uppercase in pill shape — rounded rectangle with padding

## Layout Patterns

### Cover Card
- Giant headline dominates 60–70% of card
- Small pill badge in corner (topic/series label)
- Brand mark bottom-right (small)
- Optional: repeating background text (marquee/watermark effect)

### Content / List Card
- Numbered or bulleted list
- Bold number + medium text
- Clean spacing, navy or white background
- Small heading at top for context

### Stats / Numbers Card
- 1–3 huge numbers centered
- Tiny label below each number
- High contrast, bright palette

### Quote / Definition Card
- Large quote or term, centered
- Source or context below in smaller text
- Minimal design, lots of whitespace

### CTA Card
- Bold action verb as headline (ЗАПИСАТЬСЯ / НАЧНИ / ПОЛУЧИ)
- Supporting 1-line subtext
- Bright blue background

### Step Card
- "Шаг N" badge top-left
- Bold short title
- 2–3 lines of body text
- Illustration or icon optional

## Repeating Text / Marquee Effect
Stack the same word/phrase multiple times at an angle or repeated horizontally.
Creates texture without images. Used as background layer behind main text.
Color: slightly darker or lighter shade of the background — subtle.
